About Us


Neel Dairy, your trusted source for dairy delights in Pune, brings you a collection of pure and fresh products straight from the heart of Maharashtra. Our commitment to quality and tradition ensures that every Neel product, from creamy milk to indulgent treats, is a celebration of the rich dairy heritage Pune has to offer. Join us in savoring the simplicity and goodness of Neel Dairy – where freshness meets flavor, and every bite is a taste of Pune's culinary legacy.
